# Academic Writing with Copilot: Co-evolve Your Research Judgment

_Use AI to strengthen your writing **and** your thinking — not one at the expense of the other._

## Welcome

AI writing assistants like GitHub Copilot can accelerate academic writing. But the goal of research is not just a polished manuscript — it's a sharper mind. This exercise trains you to use AI as a sparring partner that pushes **your** judgment forward, rather than a ghostwriter that replaces it.

- **Who is this for**: Graduate students, researchers, and academics who want to use AI responsibly in their writing workflow.
- **What you'll learn**: How to articulate a research question, use AI-assisted drafting with reflective critique, structure an argument, and iterate with critical judgment.
- **What you'll build**: A short academic writing portfolio — a research question, an AI-assisted draft with your reflections, and an annotated outline.
- **Prerequisites**: Basic familiarity with Markdown and GitHub.
- **How long**: This exercise takes approximately 1–2 hours to complete.

In this exercise, you will:

1. Define your research question and hypotheses
2. Draft a section with AI assistance and reflect on what you changed
3. Build an annotated argument outline
4. Open a pull request, review your work, and merge
